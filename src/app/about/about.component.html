ABOUT

The qualities that best define me are creativity, the desire to improve and the ability to set goals and fight for them with enthusiasm.


I was long time ago when I started in IT. So far, I have worked as a hardware technician, support and systems administration, being part of large teams or alone carrying all the responsibility. I have been in big and small companies, and in all of these areas I have felt comfortable and I have been able to adapt. Meanwhile I was training as a sculptor, an activity that I had always liked. I decided to set myself this challenge and I ended up working on it.


After a few years developing this work, the desire to return to technology, in this case programming, came again to me. It was the next goal I set myself, so I began to learn, this time as a self-taught. It has been a long and arduous road, but it has certainly been worth it. This portfolio is a witness to it, I hope you enjoy it.
